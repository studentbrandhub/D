# Student Brand Hub

Student Brand Hub is a platform to support student-owned businesses in the Faculty of Arts. It gives selected student entrepreneurs online visibility and promotion by linking them to free website support. Registration is handled through a Google Form, so no backend is required.

## Features

- Modern, minimal, and student-friendly design
- Green theme (primary)
- Clean typography
- Mobile-first, fast-loading
- Professional but approachable look
- Subtle digital/tech graphics in background
- Impact counters (Brands Supported, Websites Built, Students Reached)
- Brand cards with website preview, owner info, and visit button
- Contact section with WhatsApp links for campaign manager, coordinator, and community
- Footer with campaign recognition

## Sections

1. **Hero Section**: Headline, subtext, registration button, human image
2. **About the Project**: Project description and credibility visual
3. **Project Impact Counter**: Editable counters for impact stats
4. **Supported Student Brands**: Brand cards with preview images, owner, and website link
5. **Register Your Brand**: Google Form registration
6. **Why This Project Matters**: Benefits list
7. **Contact Section**: Manager, coordinator, WhatsApp links
8. **Footer**: Campaign recognition and sponsor

## How to Use

- Open `index.html` in your browser to view the site
- Update brand cards and contact info in `index.html` as needed
- Change the Google Form link to your real registration form
- Replace preview images with your own brand website screenshots

## Deployment

You can deploy this static site on GitHub Pages, Netlify, Vercel, or any static hosting platform.
